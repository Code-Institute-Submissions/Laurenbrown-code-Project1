    • About Us

    Welome to the meditation club, were offer different meditation sessions to help you relax and take a step away from your busy lifstyle.

    Whilst you're here you'll learn how to handle stress, If you often feel anxious,tense or overwhelmed due to your stressful lifestyle, medition is a great way to bring peace back into your life.

    During meditation you can increase your self awarness and focus on understanding yourself and your emotions, reflecting inwardly will help you to identify and replace negativity with postivity

    Our meditation club has a great sense of community, everyone is welcome! In a world where we can often feel alone, one of the meditation clubs goals is to be a warm and welcoming place for anyone that needs to see a friendly face or have a chat over a cup of tea.

    You can receive daily affirmations and alerts when we have guest speakers before they are announced publicly on our website when you sign up to receive our news letter, all you have to do is go to our sign up page and submit your details.
